Explore Our Coffee Menu

Our comprehensive menu page helps you find your favorite coffee.

1]Americano

An Americano is a coffee made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water. It has a similar strength to traditional brewed coffee but retains the rich flavor of espresso.

RS 250

2]Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's a strong and bold coffee with a rich and intense flavor.

RS 300

3]Flarwhite

A Flat White is a coffee drink made with espresso and microfoam (steamed milk with fine, velvety bubbles). It has a strong coffee flavor balanced with the smoothness of the microfoam, creating a creamy and velvety texture.

RS350

4]Latte

A Latte is made with espresso and steamed milk. It usually has a small amount of foam on top. It has a milder coffee flavor compared to a straight espresso, with the creaminess from the steamed milk.

RS 350

5]Mocha

A Mocha is a coffee drink that combines espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup. It's like a chocolate-infused latte, offering a sweet and indulgent flavor.

RS 350
